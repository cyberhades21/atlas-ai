# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in ATLAS a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or personal attacks
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening a [private security advisory](https://github.com/cyberhades21/atlas-ai/security/advisories/new) or by contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub.

Maintainers will review reports confidentially and take action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from the project.
